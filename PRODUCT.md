# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Individual users looking for quick, daily expense logging (rushed or focused state of mind).

## Product Purpose
FinTrack is a personal expense tracker app designed to help individuals log and analyze their detailed spending history with ease, using a dashboard for visualization and AI-assisted categorization.

## Brand Personality
Professional, secure, data-dense, and premium (similar to Stripe or Linear).

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, generic SaaS templates with neon colors and oversized card grids.
- Bland, default browser-style admin panels with pure-gray borders and no visual depth.
- Overly complex or loud consumer finance apps with constant popups and gamification.

## Design Principles
- **Streamlined Efficiency**: Optimize for speed and minimal friction during daily logging when users are rushed.
- **Premium Information Density**: Render financial data clearly with clean tables, structured layouts, and intentional spacing, avoiding generic cards.
- **Calm, Executive Tone**: Use a refined color palette, subtle depth, and clear visual hierarchy to inspire security and professional confidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard WCAG AA compliance (contrast ratios ≥4.5:1 for body text, ≥3:1 for large text).
- Full support for system-wide light/dark themes with smooth transitions.
- Respect for prefers-reduced-motion with instant or crossfade alternatives for animations.
